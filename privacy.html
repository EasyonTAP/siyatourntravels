Privacy Policy

At Siya Tour N Travels, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and travel services.

1. Introduction

At Siya Tour N Travels, we recognize the importance of protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to keep it secure during your travel booking and service experience with us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing details when you make a booking or interact with our website's features.

2.1 Personal Information

When you book travel services or create an account with us, we collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and contact details
  • Billing information and payment details
  • Travel preferences and destination choices
  • Travel dates and accommodation preferences
  • Emergency contact information

2.2 Booking Information

We gather information related to your travel preferences to facilitate your booking experience:

  • Destination choices and travel dates
  • Accommodation and transportation preferences
  • Special requests or dietary requirements
  • Travel insurance preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to process and manage your bookings, send confirmations, and provide customer support throughout your travel journey.

  • Booking and Reservation: Process and manage your bookings, send confirmations, and provide customer support
  • Communication: Contact you via email or phone to update about your bookings and share relevant travel information
  • Service Improvement: Analyze website performance, identify trends, and enhance our services and user interface
  • Marketing: Inform you about promotions or special offers (only if you opt-in to receive such communications)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms of service, and protect our rights and property

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted third-party partners who assist us in delivering our travel services, but we do not sell or rent your personal information.

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted partners such as airlines, hotels, tour operators, and payment processors. These partners have access to the data required to fulfill your bookings but are not allowed to use it for other purposes.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We will share personal information if we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

  • Meet any applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • Enforce our terms of service
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Siya Tour N Travels, our customers, or others

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Secure data transmission using encryption protocols
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments and updates to our systems
  • Secure storage of payment and booking information

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information. You may:

  • Access and review the personal information we have collected about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • Restrict or object to certain processing of your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We will post the revised version with the effective date on our website. Your continued use of the website constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy, please contact us at Siya Tour N Travels using the information provided below.
